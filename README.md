ARHANSA - Healthcare Clinic Management System
ARHANSA is a comprehensive maternal and child healthcare clinic management system built with PHP and Tailwind CSS. It helps midwives and healthcare providers manage patient care, track clinical services, and generate standardized reports following Indonesian Ministry of Health guidelines.

Features
Patient Management
Digital medical records (RM) with automated numbering
Patient history tracking with SOAP format documentation
Integrated document management system
Profile photo and document uploads
Clinical Services
Maternal Care
Antenatal Care (ANC)

Standard 10T examination workflow
Pregnancy risk assessment (Poedji Rochjati Score)
Lab results tracking
Growth charts and monitoring
Intranatal Care (INC)

Labor progress monitoring
APGAR score assessment
Delivery documentation
Complications tracking
Postnatal Care (PNC)

Maternal recovery monitoring
Breastfeeding support
Vitamin A supplementation tracking
Iron tablet distribution
Child Health
Growth monitoring (weight, height, head circumference)
Development milestone tracking
Immunization records and scheduling
Nutrition assessment
Family Planning
Multiple contraceptive method support
Side effects monitoring
Follow-up scheduling
Informed consent management
Reporting & Analytics
PWS-KIA reports generation
Monthly service statistics
Patient visit trends
Export to PDF and Excel formats
