# ARHANSA - Healthcare Clinic Management System

ARHANSA is a comprehensive healthcare clinic management system designed for midwives and healthcare providers to manage maternal and child health services. Built with PHP and Tailwind CSS, it provides an intuitive interface for tracking patient care, generating reports, and ensuring compliance with Indonesian Ministry of Health standards.

![ARHANSA Logo](/assets/images/logo.png)

## Features

- **Patient Management**
  - Electronic medical records
  - Patient history tracking
  - Document management

- **Clinical Services**
  - Antenatal Care (ANC) tracking
  - Intranatal Care (INC) monitoring
  - Postnatal Care (PNC) follow-up
  - Family Planning (KB) services
  - Immunization records
  - Child growth monitoring

- **Reporting & Analytics**
  - PWS-KIA reports (Mother and Child Health Local Area Monitoring)
  - Monthly service statistics
  - Cohort analysis
  - Export to PDF and Excel

- **Standards Compliance**
  - Indonesian Ministry of Health guidelines
  - WHO healthcare standards
  - SOAP documentation format
